Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have a dental checkup?
We recommend visiting every 6 months for a routine exam and cleaning, or as advised by your dentist.
What should I do if I have a dental emergency?
Call us immediately. We offer prompt emergency appointments for urgent dental issues.
Are your treatments safe for children?
Yes, we provide gentle, age-appropriate care for children and teens.
Do you offer dental implants and cosmetic options?
Absolutely. We provide dental implants, teeth whitening, veneers, and more.
Is teeth whitening safe and effective?
Yes, our professional whitening treatments are safe, effective, and long-lasting.
